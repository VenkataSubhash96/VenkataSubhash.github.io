About Me

This page is basically about me and my name is Venkata Subhash. Hey forgot to wish you, Hi. So, I was born and brought up in visakhaptanam I did my schooling in skvm. I cannot forget those days. I was very passionate about drums. So I waited for the chance to learn. But unfortunately I did not get the chance to learn drums during my schooling. But I learned tabla in my seventh standard. I played for school prayers and songs. I also took part in some programmes on behalf of my school. So, I spent my school days playing tabla, playing with girls etc. Then I did my plus 1 and plus 2 in chaitanya college. While I was studying plus 2, I was very aware of the fact that I would not get a seat in IIT. So, I did not concentrate much on IIT but I was so desperate to leave AP and join any good reputed univerity outside AP. Since my brother aslo did his engineering at amrita, I was little partial towards amrita. Finally I joined amrita. I had a very good college life. Those were the unforgettable days. I did not give up in learning drums. So, I learned jazz for some months. Then I got a chance to play them for one event in college. And I was glad It was a successful event. I also love to play cricket and that happened as well. The time has come for placements. Just one thing is going on in my mind. I wanted a package of 6lpa and above. The came this scripbox and I was happy about that. My dream came true. The posting is in bangalore. I was new to this city. Sairam was there in scripbox to accompany me. Bangalore life is good until now. We had good weekends and the work is going well in the office. So, thats about me.
